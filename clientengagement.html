Client Engagement & Discovery Process

At the young age of 12, I embarked on an entrepreneurial journey as a DJ, starting with gigs for friends and family. Through self-promotion, marketing campaigns, and word-of-mouth referrals, I established a reputable DJ business, serving schools, colleges, and various institutions in my community. This business was more than just playing music; it was about organization, planning, and delivering exceptional service.

My process began with discovery calls where I would meet clients to understand their needs, document the service requirements, and formalize agreements through detailed contracts. These agreements outlined the services to be delivered, payment terms, and event schedules. Over time, this process evolved from handwritten notes to professionally typed contracts.

As a teenager, I facilitated events like weddings, where I took on roles as an MC and event coordinator. I managed ceremonial rituals such as the introduction of the wedding party, first dances, cake cutting, and garter removal with precision and professionalism. I ensured every stakeholder's needs were met, executing plans flawlessly and serving clients with white-glove treatment.
